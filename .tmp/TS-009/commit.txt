    security(mcp): replace Bun.env spread with targeted key merging in env.ts
    
    Spreading all of Bun.env into process.env is a security risk — it
    indiscriminately exposes every environment variable available at runtime.
    
    Replace the spread with an explicit KEYS_TO_MERGE list that only copies
    the seven specific keys the MCP server actually needs (HOLOCRON_URL,
    HOLOCRON_DEPLOY_KEY, HOLOCRON_OPENAI_API_KEY, HOLOCRON_SITE_URL,
    CONVEX_URL, CONVEX_SITE_URL, OPENAI_API_KEY).
    
    Closes TS-009
