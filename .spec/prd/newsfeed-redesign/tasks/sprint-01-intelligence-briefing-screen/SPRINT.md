# Sprint 1: Intelligence Briefing Screen

**Sequence:** 1
**Timeline:** Phase 1
**Status:** Planned

---

## Overview

Build the complete "Signal Intelligence" newsfeed from scratch: a shared color constants file, four new visual components (header, filter bar, finding card, hero card), the orchestrating screen, and a one-line route swap. When this sprint ships, the What's New screen is fully transformed from a generic subscription feed into an editorial intelligence briefing.

---

## Human Test Deliverable

Open the holocron app and navigate to What's New — the screen displays an editorial Intelligence Briefing layout with a date/freshness header, scrollable category filter bar, a prominent hero card for the top finding, and category-coded cards with left-border accents for all other findings.

**Test Steps:**
1. Open the holocron mobile app and navigate to the What's New screen via the drawer.
2. Confirm the header shows today's date (e.g., "SAT, APR 19") and a colored freshness indicator dot — green if the report was generated recently, amber if older.
3. Confirm a single hero card appears at the top with an eyebrow "TOP SIGNAL" label, enlarged bold title, and a wider left-border accent.
4. Tap the "RELEASES" filter chip and confirm only release-category cards remain visible in the list below.
5. Scroll the horizontal filter bar left and right to confirm all category chips are reachable.
6. Tap any finding card (hero or regular) and confirm a WebViewSheet opens displaying the article.
7. Pull down to refresh and confirm a loading skeleton appears while the new report generates.
8. Confirm the social "Community Pulse" group card still appears in its correct position when social findings exist.
